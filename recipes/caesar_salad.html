Caesar Salad

Caesar Salad is a classic salad that combines crisp romaine lettuce, creamy Caesar dressing, crunchy croutons, and a sprinkle of Parmesan cheese. It's a perfect side dish or a light meal on its own. Here's how you can make it at home:

Ingredients:

  • 1 head of romaine lettuce, washed and chopped
  • 1/2 cup Caesar dressing
  • 1 cup croutons
  • 1/4 cup grated Parmesan cheese
  • Optional: grilled chicken or shrimp for added protein

Instructions:

  1. In a large bowl, toss the chopped romaine lettuce with the Caesar dressing until the lettuce is evenly coated.
  2. Add the croutons and grated Parmesan cheese to the bowl and toss gently to combine.
  3. If you're adding grilled chicken or shrimp, place them on top of the salad.
  4. Serve immediately and enjoy your delicious Caesar Salad!
