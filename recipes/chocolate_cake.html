Chocolate Cake

A rich and moist chocolate cake that's perfect for any occasion. Here's how you can make it at home:

Ingredients:

  • 2 cups all-purpose flour
  • 2 cups granulated sugar
  • 3/4 cup cocoa powder
  • 2 teaspoons baking powder
  • 1 teaspoon baking soda
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 1 cup buttermilk
  • 1 cup strong black coffee, cooled
  • 1 cup vegetable oil
  • 2 large eggs
  • 2 teaspoons vanilla extract

Instructions:

  1. Preheat the oven to 350°F (175°C).
  2. In a large bowl, whisk together the flour, sugar, cocoa powder, baking powder, baking soda, and salt.
  3. In another bowl, combine the buttermilk, coffee, oil, eggs, and vanilla extract.
  4. Pour the wet ingredients into the dry ingredients and mix until just combined.
  5. Pour the batter into two 9-inch round cake pans that have been greased and floured.
  6. Bake for 30-35 minutes, or until a toothpick inserted into the center comes out clean.
  7. Cool the cakes in the pans for 10 minutes, then turn them out onto a wire rack.
  8. Frost with your favorite chocolate frosting and enjoy!
